    About Me

    Joaquin Alvarez

    Game & Level Designer

    Game & Level Designer with strong technical skills in Unity and C# scripting. Experienced in designing engaging levels, prototyping mechanics, and supporting game development with both creative and technical expertise.

    I have worked on academic, independent, and professional projects, developing everything from 2D games to 3D environments, always with the goal of creating challenging and meaningful player experiences.

    I also have a modding background: Developed polished custom levels for games such as DOOM, Quake, Counter-Strike, Super Mario World and Half-Life. Recognized for their design quality and attention to detail.
